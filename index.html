Hi. I'm Abby. I grew up in SF, Marin, and the East Bay to then find myself on the opposite side of the country for 5 years of school in Cambridge where I studied CS + Neuroscience. I am now back on the West Coast, but this time in Silicon Valley (which feels like a whole new universe within the Bay Area).

I am working in venture capital & operations for very early stage companies, which I have come to enjoy, but am still struggling to reconcile it with the years I spent getting my engineering degree. I spent 4 out of my 5 years at school on a D1 NCAA rowing team as well, teaching me so much of what I know about leadership, commitment, and hard work.

I love feeling strong & participating in sport of any kind; I am still figuring out what this looks like without a team of my best friends and mandatory warmup time of 6:20am everyday, but I have found a lot of fun in casually running and challenging myself to learn tennis.

I also think it's fun to observe the fashion and beauty industries from the sideline. I could eat self-serve frozen yogurt for every meal, and I am a big fan of Noah Kahan, Tim McGraw, and Adele.

This is where I put all of it.
